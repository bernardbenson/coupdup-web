Help — quick guides & FAQs

Frequently asked

Short answers to help you get started fast.

Do I need to create an account?

No extra accounts. Sign in with your Apple ID or Gmail; your data syncs privately with iCloud.

How do location reminders work?

Assign one or more stores and choose a distance. CoupdUp alerts you as you arrive.

Can I keep barcodes digitally?

Yes. Store barcodes with each coupon so the cashier can scan from your phone.

Will it drain my battery?

CoupdUp is optimized for iOS power management and performs work only when helpful.

Which iPhones are supported?

iOS 16 and later. For the best experience, use the latest iOS version.

Privacy, by design

Your information stays on your devices and in your private iCloud. We only ask for permissions that unlock features you choose to use (camera for scanning, location for nearby alerts, notifications for reminders).

  • Sign in with Apple ID or Gmail
  • iCloud sync on your account
  • Clear permission prompts
